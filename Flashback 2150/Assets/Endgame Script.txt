Arnold sees a dark room ahead of him and walks in.
As he does, the lights suddenly turn on to reveal stacks of barrels filling the room.
A barrel of front of Arnold lays open, and out of curiosity he looks inside.
A thick, black liquid fills the barrel. Before he can get a closer look, he hears a voice from behind him
"You shouldn't be here you know"
Arnold turns around to a magnificient horror.
A man, oozing black liquid from his pores, with skin white as snow and eyes dark as night, stood before Arnold.
"Time to rest now, we are coming soon ..."
Too shocked to do anything, Arnold just stands there as the man approaches him, arm extended ...
